<h3 class="jsx-1311002414">Who are we looking for</h3>
<div class="jsx-1311002414">
<p>We are looking for an experienced and motivated Product Designer who is passionate about creating <strong>practical, simple and human</strong> solutions to complex problems which will help design the future of Evocon \xe2\x80\x93 a product used by manufacturing companies worldwide. <br>
<br>The Product Designer of Evocon serves an important role by creating <strong>simple, user-friendly and engaging experiences</strong>, continuously improving the user journey, and crafting every product detail. <br>
<br>Your success will be defined not only by the quality of your design work but by the satisfaction of end-users.&nbsp;</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="jsx-1311002414 vacancy-details__section">
<h3 class="jsx-1311002414">Qualifications</h3>
<div class="jsx-1311002414">
<ul>
<li>A minimum of 4 years of experience designing and shipping digital products and systems.</li>
<li>Ability in the entire product development process, from discovery through to delivery and monitoring of the solution.</li>
<li>A natural inclination to always think MVP first.</li>
<li>Not only technically savvy but the ability to understand technical constraints and their impact and to adjust the design approach accordingly.</li>
<li>Experience in user testing sessions and validating design efforts.</li>
<li>Can prototype ideas quickly and iterate as many times as needed using tools of your preference.</li>
<li>HTML / CSS knowledge considered an asset.</li>
<li>Desire to work in a respectful, mature, and collaborative work environment.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div class="jsx-1311002414 vacancy-details__section">
<h3 class="jsx-1311002414">Responsibilities</h3>
<div class="jsx-1311002414">
<ul>
<li>Create thoughtful and appropriate solutions to design challenges of all sizes by generating research insights and translating them into simple and engaging user interfaces which satisfy user needs, company requirements and technical constraints.</li>
<li>Be part of the product and design team and collaborate with them - from discovery to delivery, on to monitoring after release.</li>
<li>Design engaging experiences in an iterative manner, using flow diagrams, sketches and prototypes, taking care to validate your concepts along the way.</li>
<li>Translate validated concepts into design handouts and provide the development team with the design support they need.</li>
<li>Continue to develop Evocon\'s internal style and brand guidelines for UI/UX, whilst contributing to the maintenance and improvement of such guidelines.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
